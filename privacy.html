Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Your Terraform code and infrastructure configurations never leave your browser. InfraSketch processes everything client-side. We do not store, transmit, or have access to any code you paste into the tool.

Overview

InfraSketch ("we", "our", "the tool") is a free, open-source infrastructure diagramming tool available at infrasketch.cloud. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle data.

What data we collect

InfraSketch is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here is what we do and do not collect:

Code you paste into the tool: We do NOT collect, store, transmit, or log any Terraform, Docker Compose, CloudFormation, or other infrastructure code that you input into InfraSketch. All parsing and diagram generation happens entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. Your code never touches our servers.

Diagrams you generate: Diagrams are rendered in your browser and are not sent to any server. When you export a diagram as PNG or SVG, the file is generated locally and downloaded directly to your device.

Usage analytics: We may use privacy-respecting analytics (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site — including pages visited, time on site, browser type, and country. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.

Advertising: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google's Ads Settings (https://adssettings.google.com).

Cookies

InfraSketch itself does not set any cookies. However, third-party services we use (Google Analytics, Google AdSense) may set cookies on your browser. These cookies are governed by the respective privacy policies of those services.

  • Google Analytics Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdSense Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — for displaying advertisements
  • Google Analytics — for understanding site usage patterns
  • GitHub Pages — for hosting the website

Data security

Since we do not collect or store your infrastructure code, there is no sensitive data at risk. The website is served over HTTPS to ensure all communications between your browser and our site are encrypted.

Children's privacy

InfraSketch is not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Opt out of personalized advertising via Google's Ads Settings
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings
  • Use browser extensions to block analytics and advertising scripts
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of InfraSketch after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at infrasketch.cloud@gmail.com or visit our Contact page.
